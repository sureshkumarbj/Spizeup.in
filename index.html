Why Choose Spize up

Why Indian small cardamom is superior?

Export quality of cardamom from India, Guatemala and Srilanka were characterized by Jaleel Kizhakkayil et.al . (Natural product radiance, vol 5(5), 2006, pp. 361-365.). For most of the physical parameters such as weight of 100 capsules (24.26g) and for bio chemical traits such as starch and crude fibre Indian cardamom is found superior to other producers. Further Indian small cardamom indicates higher quantities of alpha-terpinyl acetate and 1,8 - cineole, which impart aroma and flavour to the cardamom, thus reinforcing legendary belief of high intrinsic quality of the Indian cardamom.

E.cardamom is considered to be originated in western ghats of India from where it has spreaded over to other countries.Presently called Idukki district is situated in Western ghats. Major share nearly 75% of indian small cardamom comes from Idukki district. Bodinayakanur is in the border of Idukki district and also its the market capital for Indian small cardamom. We are operating from Bodinayakanur. So we can supply fresh and best quality of cardamom.

Key Factors

  • Cardamom (E. cardamomum) is an aromatic herb from the family Zingiberaceae with a pleasant aroma and a characteristic, pungent taste.
  • In the Gulf region, cardamom is commonly employed as flavoring ingredient for coffee and tea, while in Saudi Arabia, the largest consumer in the world, it is mainly used for preparation of Gahwa.
  • Cardamom possesses multiple traditional pharmaceutical uses.
  • Cardamom contains considerable amounts of valuable natural antioxidants and volatile constituents with multiple bioactivities.
